# Virada

Virada is a modern AI-powered stock news analysis platform. It reads market news, classifies each headline as positive, negative, or neutral, and turns noisy information into simple stock movement signals.

## What Virada Does

Financial markets move fast. News can create hype, panic, and emotional decisions.

Virada helps users understand the likely market direction by analyzing news and separating signal from noise.

## Core Idea

Virada classifies market news into three categories:

- **Positive** — the news may support a price increase
- **Negative** — the news may support a price decrease
- **Neutral** — the news may not cause major price movement

The platform is designed to reduce bias and make financial news easier to understand.

## Current Accuracy

Virada currently reaches approximately **85% directional accuracy** in internal testing.

## Features

- AI-powered news sentiment analysis
- Positive, negative, and neutral signal classification
- Bias-reduction layer
- Clean stock movement predictions
- Modern landing page design
- Responsive layout for desktop and mobile
